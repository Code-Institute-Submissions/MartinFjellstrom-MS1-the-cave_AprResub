Competitions

Info

At The Cave we host climbing competitions a few times a year for all of our members and local climbers. These are suitable for all climbing abilities and are an opportunity for climbers to battle it out for some exciting prizes as well as get a taste for competitive climbing.
Competitions are held in two categories, Top Rope climbing and Bouldering. Within both categories climbers are separated into levels.
If you enjoy climbing and want to test your skills whilst having some fun, sign up for one of our upcoming competitions using the link below. Participation is free for members and an entry fee is required for non-members.
If you are not yet ready to compete but would still like to get involved, you can also help out as a volunteer! For more information about this please contact us by email (email) or on our contact page (link).
Everyone is welcome to come and spectate and cheer the climbers on!

Rules

Rope Competition

Each climber will do a total of 8 climbs and receive 100 points for completing a route. Climbers are given a second attempt to complete routes. Additional points are given for completing routes on the first attempt.

Bouldering Competition
