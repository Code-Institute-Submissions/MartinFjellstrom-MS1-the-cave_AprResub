THE CAVE

Welcome to The Cave!

We are a climbing facility located in the heart of Gothenburg. Whether you are here for the first time or an experienced climber looking for a place to practice, we have something for everyone!



About Us

At The Cave we have walls suitable for top rope climbing, lead climbing and bouldering. Our walls are between 10-15.5 meters high. Our facility boasts vertical and overhang climbing walls as well as an artificial rock wall and a bouldering section. If you are interested in getting to know more about climbing we also offer courses and host competitions. Have a look through our site to find out more!
