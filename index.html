Welcome to The Cave!

We are a climbing facility located in the heart of Gothenburg. Everyone is welcome here, whether you are a beginner and trying for the first time or an experienced climber. We have walls of various difficulties.

About Us

At The Cave we have walls suitable for both top rope climbing, lead climbing and bouldering. Our walls are between 10-15.5 meters high. Here you have the opportunity to try out vertical and overhang climbing as well as an artificial rock wall and a bouldering section. We also offer courses and host competitions. For more information follow the links in the top right hand menu.
