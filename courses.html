Courses

We have courses for everyone whether you are a beginner or an experienced climber looking to improve your technique.


Top Rope

If you are a beginner, our Top Rope course is a great place to start. The course is designed to help you understand all the basics of climbing and is divided into two different parts. You will be in a small group of 8 people (maximum) and all the necessary gear will be provided by us. After a successful course you will be issued a Green Card which will allow you to rent gear and climb in all the climbing facilities in Sweden.

Part 1

The first part of the course is 1,5 hours long. During this part you will get to know the gear, buddy checks and how to climb in a safe way. You will then be able to practice your knowledge under controlled circumstances.

Part 2

During this part you will continue to practice your knowledge about how to secure the gear, and climb alongside your course buddies and leader for 1,5 hours. In the last 10 minutes we will test your knowledge with a theory test. If you do well in this and the training you will receive The Green Card.



Lead

To be able to take part in our Lead course, you will need to have a Green card and be able to climb at Top Rope 6a level. The course duration is two hours. You will be in a group of 6 people (max) and will practice lead securing/climbing, and falling techniques. All necessary gear will be provided by us. After a successful course you will receive The Red Card which will allow you to climb lead walls in most climbing facilities in Sweden.

The course starts by going through the basics of climbing gear and essential knots. After that we will be doing some lead securing and climbing on two different walls. The last 30 minutes we will consist of learning different falling techniques and how to fall in a safe way. If everything goes well you will receive The Red Card.


Technique

Our Technique courses are for both beginners and experienced climbers who want to develop their climbing techniques. The course will take place over a few days and will be tailored to the group's needs. You will come out of this course with lots of useful tips and exercises to practice on your own afterwards. You will need to bring your own gear or hire it from us.

Beginner

The beginner course is suitable for first time climbers. The course will take place over 3 days. You will learn the basics as well as some more advanced techniques. You will also learn how to get started training to continuously develop your skills. The main focus is Top Rope climbing but also includes some bouldering.

Advanced

The advanced course is designed for more experienced climbers who climb at level 6c-7b. This course takes place over 5 days and focuses on more advanced climbing and movement techniques. You will learn how to train to become an even better climber. During the course you will also receive exercises to try out on your own.
