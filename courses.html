Courses

We have courses for everyone whether you are a beginner or an experienced climber looking to improve your technique.


If you just started climbing our Top Rope course is what's suitable for you where you don't need any previous experience. The course will help you understand all the basics of climbing and is divided into two different occasions. You will be in a small group of max 8 people and all the gear needed will be provided by us. After a successful course you will be given The Green Card which allows you to rent gear and climb in all climbing facilities in Sweden.

Part 1

The first part of the course is 1,5 hours long. Here you will get to know the gear, buddy checks and how to climb in a safe way. During this time you will be allowed to practice your knowledge under controlled circumstances.

Part 2

During this part you will continue to practice your knowledge in how to secure the gear and climb together with you course buddies and leader for 1,5 hours. The last 10 minutes consist of a theory test and if that together with your training goes well you will receive The Green Card.



To be able to take part in our Lead course, you need o have a Green card and be able to climb Top Rope 6a difficulty. This course take place at one occasion for two hours. You will be in a group of max 6 people and will together try out lead securing/climbing, and falling techniques. All the gear needed will be provided by us. After a successful course you will receive a The Red Card which will allow you to climb lead walls in most climbing facilities in Sweden.

The curse starts by going though the basics with the gear and certain knots that we will be using. After that we will be doing some lead securing and climbing on two different walls. The last 30 minutes we will be going through different falling techniques and how to fall in a safe way. If everything goes well you will receive The Red Card.


Our Technique courses are for both beginners and experienced climbers who wants to develop their climbing techniques. The course takes place at multiple occasions and we will focus on what you and your group needs to focus on. You will come out of this course with loads of tips and exercises to practice on your own afterwards. To this course you need to bring your own gear or rent it with us.

Beginner

The beginner course is for you who just started climbing. The course will take pace on 3 different occasions. During this course you will learn the basics and a bit more advanced techniques and how to get your training started the best way to continuously develop you future skills. We will focus on Top Rope climbing but we will also do some bouldering.

Advanced

The advanced course is for you who have been climbing for a while and climbs around 6c-7b. This course will take place during 5 different occasions. We will focus on more advanced climbing and movement techniques. Also get a closer look on how you should train to become an even better climber. Between the occasions you will receive exercises to try out on your own.




To participate in our courses you need to be 15 years old and if you re under 18 we will need your parents signature.
